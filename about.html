Origin Story

Our story started 15 years ago when we traveled to Thailand. We tasted the authentic local cuisine and instantly fell in love. When we returned to the states, we vowed to open our own Thai restaurant to share this wonderful food with others. Our Raleigh location is the flagship store, because our home is in North Carolina and this was such a wonderful location.
